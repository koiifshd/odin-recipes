Recipe for Rice (Jasmine)

Description

If you are Asian, and you require this recipe to cook rice, I am sorry. Are you okay? Anyways, let's get started!

Ingredients

  • 1 cup for each serving (recommended to cook >3 cups for a family)
  • Same amount of (preferably drinking) water as rice
  • Water for Washing Rice
  • Ultimate Tool: Rice Cooker

    • Instructions

    1. Count how many servings you need, and put the amount of rice you need in the pot-like metal bowl that comes with the rice cooker. Here, you will rinse the rice 3 times, and make sure the rice is at least considerably clean. Use both hands to knead the rice. After each rinse, dump out the "dirty" water (It should be milky white).
    2. After 3 washes of the rice, add the preferably drinking water to the rice with a 1 to 1 ratio of water to rice.
    3. Make sure to use a towel or anything to completely dry off the exterior of the rice pot.
    4. Put the filled rice pot into the rice cooker (make sure the plug is off first).
    5. Close the top lid of the rice cooker and plug its cord into the socket.
    6. Press the Start button (there should be one) and pray that your parents come home after the rice is finished cooking.
    7. After the rice is finished cooking, open the rice cooker and use a rice spoon to ladle the cooked rice into a bowl or onto a plate.
    8. Congratulations! You have made one of the basic necessities of life! Hopefully your parents didn't come home before the rice was cooked!
    9. Put other stuff on top of rice or in other plates.
    10. Enjoy!
